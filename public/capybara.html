The Problem

Creating, maintaining and growing a website is hard work, it takes a lot of time and the costs can mount up quickly.

Many websites and services rely on showing ads and/or selling your personal data. Both things PerfectQuran is going to avoid.

When PerfectQuran was first created we had no idea how popular it was going to be. Without any real means to pay for the growing costs, that popularity turned out to be too expensive and eventually killed the site.
